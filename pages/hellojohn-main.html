About

Warm. Hearty. Unpretentious.

Hello John is the food side of our shared space in TTDI Jaya, Shah Alam. While Blank Note Coffee takes care of your drinks, we take care of the rest - from proper mains to late-night snacks.

Named after no one in particular, and everyone who's ever said "what should we eat?" - Hello John is an answer to that.
