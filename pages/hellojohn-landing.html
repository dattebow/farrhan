Hello John

hellojohn ↗

Warm. Hearty. Unpretentious.

Hello John is the food side of a shared lot in TTDI Jaya, Shah Alam - operating alongside Blank Note Coffee. While Blank Note handles the drinks, Hello John handles everything else.

Named after no one in particular and everyone who's ever asked "what should we eat?" - this is the companion site to the Blank Note Coffee project, built as an extension of the same assignment.

FAQ

What does the site include?

A menu split into mains, snacks, and drinks - plus an info page with hours, location, and quick-action buttons for reservations, delivery, and event catering.

How does it connect to Blank Note Coffee?

The two sites cross-link each other. Hello John's drinks tab points to BNC for coffee, and BNC's food tab points back to Hello John.

What's it built with?

HTML and CSS. Hosted under farrhan.com using the Frangipani and Nero colour palette with Inter as the typeface.
