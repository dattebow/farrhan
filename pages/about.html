About Me

Hi! I'm Farrhan, though many know me as Dip. I'm a video editor and designer based in Kuala Lumpur. It started in 2014 with nothing more than a few funny voice call recordings and a YouTube channel - all of my old videos are deleted, but you can try your best to find them.

These days I'm a full-time video editor at Grass Konbini, a media and marketing company. On the side I edit for VTubers and live streamers - the brief usually sits somewhere between corporate polish and the controlled chaos of shitposting, and figuring out where to land is the part I enjoy most. Working across those styles keeps things from getting stale.

I run a YouTube channel called dattebow too. Long-form essay videos about games, tech, whatever I feel like making. No schedule, no trend-chasing. Just videos I'd want to watch.

Outside of editing I shoot film and digital photography, play bass, and tinker with tech more than is probably necessary. The JRPG backlog is a problem I've made peace with.

I've been at this since 2014 and I'm not stopping anytime soon.
