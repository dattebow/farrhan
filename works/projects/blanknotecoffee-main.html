About

Urban. Industrial. Casual.

Located in TTDI Jaya, Shah Alam - Blank Note Coffee was established in 2024 as a side project.

We designed this cafe for both focus and connection. Our space is surprisingly quiet even though it's right next to the highway. Whether you're here to get some work done or catch up with friends, we provide the blank canvas you need to unwind.
