dattebow

youtube.com/@dattebow ↗

Most of my friends play games- just not the same ones as me. And sometimes you want to talk about something more than just "yeah that game was cool." So I made a YouTube channel. Started out of boredom, turned into an outlet for talking about games, tech, and whatever else is on my mind. I like everything about making content, so here we are.

FAQ

Why did you start dattebow??

I like making content. And because I was bored. No grand vision, no five-year plan. I was just bored.

How often do you upload??

Not often. Only when I feel like it. Who knows? Maybe one day I'll have a proper schedule for it

What's your setup / how do you make your videos?

It starts in Obsidian where I script everything out. A-roll and B-roll are shot using either an action camera or a mirrorless (full gear list on the equipment page). Everything gets stitched together in Adobe Premiere Pro. Then something magical happens. And then BOOM- video's up. Something like that.
